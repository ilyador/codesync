A

Treat entire chain as one slot

The chain occupies a single "slot" in the drop calculation. Cursor above the group → drop before it. Cursor below → drop after it. The group's midpoint is the center of the entire chain.

B

Skip chain internals, show indicator at edges

Drop indicator appears above the first task or below the last task in the chain. When cursor is anywhere over the chain's middle, no indicator shows (dead zone). Simpler to implement.
